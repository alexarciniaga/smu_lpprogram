Before Applying
Take a moment to review the following admission requirements and what you'll need to apply.
1. Bachelor’s degree in statistics, applied mathematics, computer science, engineering or other quantitative disciplines
2. Experience with a programming language (R, JAVA, C++, Python, etc.)
3. At least two courses completed in calculus (i.e., Calculus I & II)
4. GRE required *
* Exception: A minimum of two years of relevant work experience in a technology field will waive the GRE requirement and may substitute for other prerequisite requirements pending faculty approval.
